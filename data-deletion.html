1 What Data We Hold

Depending on how you used SupportGent, we may hold the following data about you:

  • Agent accounts: Email address, name, business name, hashed password, billing records, agent configuration, knowledge base content
  • WhatsApp messages: Your phone number, message text, timestamps — if a business used SupportGent to respond to your WhatsApp messages
  • Instagram DMs: Your Instagram user ID, message text, timestamps — if a business used SupportGent to respond to your DMs
  • Gmail: Email content from your connected Google account (Agents only)
  • OAuth tokens: Encrypted Meta, Google access tokens (Agents only)
  • AI usage logs: Token consumption metrics (Agents only)

4 What Cannot Be Deleted

Certain data may be retained despite a deletion request due to legal obligations:

  • Financial records: Billing and payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law (GST Act)
  • Legal holds: Data subject to an active legal proceeding, court order, or regulatory investigation
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, de-identified analytics data that cannot be linked to you

We will notify you if any portion of your data cannot be deleted and explain the legal basis for retention.

5 Meta App Data Deletion Callback

This page serves as the registered Data Deletion Callback URL for SupportGent's Meta App. This is required by Meta's Platform Terms for apps that use Facebook Login or access Meta user data.

When a user removes SupportGent from their Facebook App Settings, Meta sends a deletion signal to the following URL:

Meta Data Deletion Callback URL
https://supportagent.aasaitech.in/data-deletion.html

Upon receiving this signal, we initiate an automatic deletion of all Meta platform data associated with that user's Facebook User ID, including WhatsApp messages, Instagram DMs, and Meta OAuth tokens stored in our system.

The deletion is completed within 30 days and logged in our compliance records.
