1 Who We Are

SupportGent is an AI-powered customer support platform developed and operated by Aasai Tech Providers Private Limited, a company incorporated in India (CIN: U63122TN2025PTC180265).

SupportGent enables businesses ("Agents") to manage customer communications across Email (Gmail), WhatsApp Business, and Instagram DMs from a single intelligent inbox. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data for both Agents (our direct customers) and End Users (your customers who contact you via SupportGent).

By using SupportGent, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in three categories:

2.1 Account & Agent Information

  • Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register
  • Business name and agent profile information
  • Billing information (processed securely via Razorpay)
  • OAuth tokens for Gmail, WhatsApp Business API, and Instagram (stored AES-256 encrypted)

2.2 Customer Communication Data

  • Emails received in connected Gmail inboxes (sender, subject, body)
  • WhatsApp messages received via WhatsApp Business API
  • Instagram DMs received via the Instagram Messaging API
  • AI-generated draft replies and sent replies
  • Intent classification labels and confidence scores

2.3 Usage & Technical Data

  • Log data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
  • Token usage metrics (AI API calls, tokens consumed)
  • Device and session identifiers

3 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to provide and improve the SupportGent platform:

  • Displaying your customer messages in the SupportGent inbox
  • Generating AI-powered draft replies using your knowledge base
  • Classifying message intent (enquiry, order, complaint, refund, etc.)
  • Sending replies on your behalf via Gmail, WhatsApp, or Instagram
  • Filtering noise emails automatically using rule-based pre-filtering
  • Providing usage analytics and token consumption reports
  • Authenticating your account and maintaining platform security
  • Processing subscription payments via Razorpay

We do not sell your data or your customers' data to any third party. We do not use customer message content for training our own AI models.

4 Meta Platform Data (WhatsApp & Instagram)

SupportGent integrates with Meta's platforms via the WhatsApp Business API and Instagram Messaging API. By connecting these channels, you authorize us to:

  • Receive incoming WhatsApp messages sent to your registered business phone number
  • Receive incoming Instagram DMs sent to your connected Instagram Business account
  • Send messages on your behalf in response to customer inquiries
  • Store messages in our database to display in your inbox and generate AI responses

Data we receive from Meta includes:

  • Sender phone numbers (WhatsApp) or sender user IDs (Instagram)
  • Message text content
  • Message timestamps and IDs
  • Page and WABA (WhatsApp Business Account) metadata

Data usage compliance:

  • All Meta platform data is used exclusively to provide messaging inbox functionality to the Agent
  • We do not use Meta user data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of the messaging service
  • We comply with Meta's Platform Terms, the WhatsApp Business Policy, and the Instagram Platform Policy
  • End users may request deletion of their data — see Section 8 for details

Access tokens issued by Meta are stored encrypted (AES-256-CBC) in our database and are never exposed in plaintext beyond server-side API calls.

6 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Message data is retained for as long as your account remains active
  • Deleted messages: Messages dismissed or deleted within the platform are soft-deleted and permanently purged within 30 days
  • Account deletion: Upon account deletion, all associated agent data, messages, and tokens are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • End user data deletion: End users (your customers) may request deletion of their data via our Data Deletion process — see Data Deletion Policy
  • Billing records: Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law
  • Logs: Server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days

7 Data Security

We implement the following technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens stored using AES-256-CBC encryption
  • All API communications use HTTPS / TLS 1.2 or higher
  • MongoDB Atlas encryption at rest
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token expiry
  • AWS Lambda serverless infrastructure with IAM role restrictions
  • No plaintext credential storage in any system
  • Regular security reviews of API integrations

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system can guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@aasaitech.in.

8 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data — see our Data Deletion Policy for the full process
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: Disconnect channel integrations at any time from the Channels page

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@aasaitech.in with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

For Meta platform data deletion requests specifically (WhatsApp / Instagram), please use our Data Deletion Request page which is also registered with Meta as our official data deletion callback.

9 Cookies & Tracking

SupportGent uses minimal cookies solely for authentication and session management:

  • Authentication token: A JWT stored in localStorage to keep you logged in
  • Theme preference: A local preference for light/dark mode

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services that track you across websites. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tools.

10 Children's Privacy

SupportGent is a business tool intended for use by adults and business entities only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@aasaitech.in and we will delete it promptly.

Continued use of SupportGent after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
